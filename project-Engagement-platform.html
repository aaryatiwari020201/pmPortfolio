Problem Statement

The existing OSMOS billing system was outdated, inefficient, and prone to errors, leading to customer dissatisfaction and increased operational costs.

System Issues

  • High error rate in invoice generation
  • Slow processing times for payments
  • Lack of scalability to handle increasing transaction volumes
  • Poor integration with other internal systems

Business Impact

  • Increased customer support tickets related to billing issues
  • Delayed revenue recognition due to processing delays
  • High maintenance costs for the legacy system
  • Limited ability to offer flexible billing options

My Role & Responsibilities

Lead Backend Developer

API Integration

Retrospective

What Went Well

  • Clear requirements and well-defined scope
  • Strong collaboration between development and QA teams
  • Effective use of agile development methodology
